Chi Siamo / About Us

Over a Century of Foundation

Our Legacy

Founded in 1905, Colombo Costruzioni has been a cornerstone of Italian construction for over a century. We are not just builders; we are a historical institution dedicated to executing highly complex, large-scale, and architecturally ambitious projects.

Our history is built on a foundation of trust, quality, and an unwavering commitment to innovation. This legacy inspires us to collaborate with world-renowned architects, turning visionary designs into tangible, enduring landmarks.

1905

Foundation

Colombo Costruzioni is established, beginning a legacy of construction excellence in Italy.

1994

Quality Certified

Achieved ISO 9001 certification, formalizing a long-standing commitment to the highest quality standards.

2014

Bosco Verticale

Completed the iconic "Vertical Forest" in Milan, a global benchmark for sustainable architecture.

Today

Future's Heritage

Continuing to shape Italy's skyline with landmark projects like CityLife and Porta Nuova.
